Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a custom RSS feed?

1. Go to the Sources tab
2. Tap "Add RSS Feed" under Custom Feeds
3. Enter a name and the RSS URL
4. The app will validate the feed before adding it

How many custom feeds can I add?

You can add up to 5 custom RSS feeds to keep the app fast and efficient.

How do I save an article?

Open any article and tap the bookmark icon in the top-right corner. Saved articles are stored locally on your device.
