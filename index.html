Privacy Policy - Clear News

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Clear News ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices for the Clear News RSS reader application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Clear News operates entirely on your device without transmitting personal data to external servers.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Saved Articles: Stored in your device's local database
  • Feed Preferences: Your selected news sources and custom RSS feeds
  • App Settings: Notification preferences and display settings

Network Activity

Clear News only connects to:

  • Public RSS feed servers to fetch news articles
  • USGS servers for earthquake data (if enabled)

These connections are read-only and do not transmit any personal information.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are processed entirely on your device. We do not use push notification services that collect data.

Third-Party Services

Clear News does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any information with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Clear News does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app displays publicly available news content which may not be suitable for all ages.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally and accessible through the app
  • Deletion: Delete saved articles or remove the app to delete all data
  • Portability: Your data remains on your device

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. We recommend using device passcodes and keeping your iOS updated.

International Users

Clear News is available globally (except mainland China). The app operates the same way regardless of location, storing all data locally on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Check this page for the latest version. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
